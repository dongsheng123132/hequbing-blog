Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

This page covers mobile apps published by Hequbing. We design small, focused apps that avoid accounts, ads, cross-app tracking, and unnecessary server collection.

Dopamine Tax Detox

Dopamine Tax Detox helps users pause before opening distracting apps. It works with iOS Shortcuts automation and stores usage-related data locally on the user's device.

Data collection

Dopamine Tax Detox does not collect personal data, does not require an account, does not run third-party advertising SDKs, and does not use cross-app tracking.

On-device data

The app may store local settings, selected app names, resistance counts, wait steps, and optional reasons written by the user. This information remains on the device and is not sent to servers controlled by us.

iOS Shortcuts

The app can be used with iOS Shortcuts automation so that a pause screen appears when the user opens a target app. Shortcut automation is configured by the user on their own device.

Sharing and retention

Because Dopamine Tax Detox does not upload personal data to our servers, we do not sell, share, or retain that data on our servers.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

SunRoom AI

SunRoom AI is designed to perform sunlight estimation on the user's iPhone. No account is required. We do not serve ads or use cross-app tracking.

Device permissions

  • Camera: Used to measure the window and floor and to draw the sunlight-depth line in AR. Camera imagery is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Location while using the app: Used on-device to calculate solar position. We do not retain location history or send it to servers controlled by us.
  • Device orientation and motion: Used on-device to determine window direction and stabilize AR content.

Purchases and subscriptions

If an app offers purchases or subscriptions, Apple processes them through the App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive or store payment-card details.

Contact

If app functionality or data practices change, this page and its effective date will be updated. Contact hefangsheng@gmail.com with privacy questions.
