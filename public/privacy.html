晒房 AI / SunRoom AI 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 8 日

晒房 AI 的设计原则是尽量在你的 iPhone 本地完成计算。我们不要求注册账号，不投放广告，也不使用跨 App 跟踪技术。

我们使用的设备权限

  • 相机：用于识别窗户、地板，并在 AR 画面中绘制阳光深度线。相机画面不会上传到我们的服务器。
  • 使用 App 时的位置：用于在设备本地计算太阳方位和高度。我们不保存位置历史，也不将位置发送给我们控制的服务器。
  • 设备方向与运动：用于确定窗户朝向和稳定 AR 内容，仅在设备本地处理。

购买与订阅

订阅由 Apple App Store 和 StoreKit 处理。我们只能读取 Apple 提供的购买状态以解锁功能，不接收或保存你的银行卡资料。

数据收集、分享与保留

当前版本不收集个人数据，不包含第三方广告或分析 SDK，也不会出售或分享个人信息。由于我们不在服务器保存相机、位置或房间测量数据，因此没有相应的服务器保留期。

儿童隐私

本 App 面向一般用户，不以儿童为目标，也不会有意收集儿童个人信息。

政策更新与联系

功能或数据处理方式发生变化时，我们会更新本页面及生效日期。问题可发送至 hefangsheng@gmail.com。

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026

SunRoom AI is designed to perform its calculations on your iPhone. No account is required. We do not serve ads or use cross-app tracking.

Device permissions

  • Camera: Used to measure the window and floor and to draw the sunlight-depth line in AR. Camera imagery is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Location while using the app: Used on-device to calculate solar position. We do not retain location history or send it to servers controlled by us.
  • Device orientation and motion: Used on-device to determine window direction and stabilize AR content.

Purchases and subscriptions

Apple processes subscriptions through the App Store and StoreKit. The app reads entitlement status to unlock features. We do not receive or store payment-card details.

Collection, sharing, and retention

The current version does not collect personal data, include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, sell personal information, or share it with third parties. Camera, location, and room measurements are not retained on our servers.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes and contact

If app functionality or data practices change, this page and its effective date will be updated. Contact hefangsheng@gmail.com with privacy questions.
