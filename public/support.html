Dopamine Tax Detox

Dopamine Tax Detox helps you pause before opening distracting apps. It uses small friction steps such as breathing, waiting, or writing down a reason before opening a target app.

Quick setup

  1. Open Dopamine Tax Detox and choose the apps you want to make harder to open.
  2. Follow the built-in Shortcuts setup guide.
  3. Create an iOS Shortcuts automation for each target app.
  4. When the target app opens, let the automation launch Dopamine Tax Detox first.

Troubleshooting

The pause screen does not appear: Open the Shortcuts app and confirm the automation is enabled and set to run immediately.

The wrong app is shown: Reopen Dopamine Tax Detox, check the controlled apps list, and update the matching Shortcut automation.

I want to stop using it: Disable or delete the automation in the Shortcuts app. You can also remove controlled apps in Dopamine Tax Detox.
